About us

At Keltalz Technologies, our mission is to empower individuals with knowledge and skills that open doors to endless possibilities.

We offer comprehensive training programs in UI/UX design, graphics design, video editing and animations, and basic computer practical training.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced professional, our courses cater to a diverse range of skill levels, providing a solid foundation for success in the digital world. In addition to training,

Keltalz Technologies is evolving into a full-service tech agency. We now provide cutting-edge software development services, including:

  • Website development
  • Web and mobile app creation (Android and iOS)
  • Custom business solutions

Our team of experts works with small businesses, schools, NGOs, and organizations to deliver innovative solutions that help them thrive in today's fast-paced digital landscape.

We've had the privilege of working with government organizations, NGOs, and top businesses, solidifying our reputation as a reliable and results-driven tech partner.
At Keltalz Tech Hub, we believe in hands-on learning and practical solutions. With a physical hub located in Enugu, Nigeria, we are always accessible to our clients and students. Our commitment goes beyond teaching—we help businesses and individuals create impactful digital products.
